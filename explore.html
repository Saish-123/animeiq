Frequently Asked Questions

📺 What is filler in anime?

Filler episodes are anime-only content not part of the original manga storyline. They're created to give the manga time to stay ahead.

🎯 Does AnimeIQ show all episodes?

Yes — we map canon, filler, and mixed episodes so you can choose exactly what to watch and what to skip.

⚡ Can I skip filler episodes?

Absolutely. Use the filter buttons inside any anime detail to instantly view only canon episodes.

🚀 Is AnimeIQ free to use?

Yes, AnimeIQ is completely free and built to enhance your anime watching experience. No subscriptions needed.
