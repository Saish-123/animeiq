01 👋

Introduction

Overview

Welcome to AnimeIQ. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services. By accessing AnimeIQ, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to this policy.

✅ AnimeIQ is built on trust. We believe in radical transparency — this document is written in plain language so you always know exactly what's happening with your data.
02 📊

Information We Collect

Data Collection

We collect only what we need to provide you with the best anime discovery experience. This includes personal data such as your name and email address when you create an account or contact us, and non-personal data including browser type, device information, IP address, and anonymized usage analytics to help us improve the platform.

💡 Your watchlist and favourites are stored locally on your device via localStorage and synced via Firebase if you're logged in. We never access this data for advertising purposes.
03 ⚙️

How We Use Your Information

Usage

We use your data exclusively to deliver and improve AnimeIQ's services — responding to your inquiries, personalizing your experience, analyzing anonymized usage patterns to make the platform better, and communicating important updates. Your data is never sold to third parties under any circumstances.

🚫 We do not sell, rent, lease, or trade your personal information to any external party. Ever. Full stop.
04 ⚖️

Legal Basis (GDPR)

Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal data on the following legal bases under GDPR Article 6: Legitimate Interests (providing and improving our services), Consent (for optional cookies and marketing communications), and Legal Obligation (when required by applicable law). You may withdraw consent at any time.

🇪🇺 GDPR rights apply to all EEA residents. If you are located in India, PDPA and IT Act provisions also apply to how we handle your data.
05 🍪

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Cookies

We use essential cookies to ensure the platform functions correctly (session management, login state, preferences). We may also use analytics cookies to understand aggregate usage patterns. Third-party services such as Google AdSense may place their own cookies to serve contextually relevant advertisements. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings at any time.

⚠️ Blocking essential cookies may affect the functionality of AnimeIQ features including login, watchlist saving, and personalized settings.
06 🔗

Third-Party Services

Integrations

AnimeIQ integrates with carefully selected third-party providers to deliver a seamless experience. These include Jikan API (MyAnimeList data — no personal data sent), Firebase by Google (authentication and data storage), EmailJS (contact form delivery), and potentially Google AdSense (ad delivery). Each provider operates under their own privacy policies and data protection standards.

🛡️ All third-party providers we work with are GDPR-compliant and subject to data processing agreements. We only share data that is strictly necessary for the service to function.
07 🛡️

Data Security

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data including HTTPS encryption for all data in transit, Firebase's enterprise-grade security infrastructure for data at rest, and strict access controls limiting who within our team can access user data. Authentication is handled exclusively by Firebase, meaning we never store raw passwords.

🔐 Passwords are never stored in plaintext. Firebase handles all authentication using secure, salted hashing — your credentials are protected at the infrastructure level.
08 ✊

Your Rights

Your Control

You have full control over your personal data. Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the following rights which you can exercise by contacting us at any time:

  • 👁️ Right to Access
  • ✏️ Right to Rectification
  • 🗑️ Right to Erasure
  • ⏸️ Right to Restrict Processing
  • 📦 Right to Data Portability
  • 🚫 Right to Object
  • 🔙 Right to Withdraw Consent
  • 📣 Right to Lodge a Complaint
09 👶

Children's Privacy

Age Restriction

AnimeIQ is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at support@animeiq.com and we will take immediate steps to remove that information from our systems.

⚠️ If you are under 13 years old, please do not create an account or submit any personal information on AnimeIQ.
10 🔄

Changes to This Policy

Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make material changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you via email or a prominent notice on our website. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.

📬 Significant updates to our privacy practices will always be communicated proactively to registered users. We will never silently change how we use your data.

Get in Touch
