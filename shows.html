Frequently Asked Questions

📋 What is the A–Z anime list?

It's a complete alphabetical directory of anime series in AnimeIQ, powered by live MyAnimeList data via the Jikan API.

🔍 How do I find a specific anime?

Use the search bar (or press /) to instantly filter titles, or click any letter to jump there.

📺 What happens when I click an anime?

You'll see the full detail page: synopsis, score, studio, genres, and a complete episode guide with filler detection.

⚡ Can I skip filler episodes?

Yes! Each detail page shows episodes colour-coded as Canon, Filler, or Mixed. Filter with one click to watch only what matters.

🚀 Is AnimeIQ free to use?

Yes — 100% free, always. No subscription, no paywall. AnimeIQ is built for anime fans by anime fans.

⏱️ How long will it take me to watch?

Use our Binge Calculator to find out exactly how many hours any anime will take based on your daily watch limit.
