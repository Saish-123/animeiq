Frequently Asked Questions

📺 What does this calculator do?

It calculates total watch time and the number of days needed to finish an anime, based on how many episodes you watch per day.

⚡ Is the data accurate?

Yes. Episode counts and durations are fetched live from MyAnimeList via the Jikan API — the most comprehensive anime database available.

🎯 Can I use it for long anime?

Absolutely. Long-running anime like One Piece (1000+ eps) and Naruto Shippuden are fully supported and give accurate results.

🚀 Is AnimeIQ free?

Yes — the binge calculator and all AnimeIQ features are completely free. No sign-up required to use the calculator.

🔍 How do I skip filler episodes?

Use our Filler Episode Guide on the Explore page. It maps every episode as Canon, Filler, or Mixed so you only watch what matters.

📋 Can I share my binge plan?

Yes! Hit the Copy Result button after calculating to copy your full binge plan summary and share it with friends.
