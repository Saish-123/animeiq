Common Questions

Before You Write — Check This

📺 The episode count for an anime is wrong — how do I report it? ▾
Select "Content Issue" in the message category, then tell us the anime name and the correct episode count. We pull data from MyAnimeList via the Jikan API — if MAL has it wrong, you can also correct it directly on MAL.org and it'll sync to us.
🐛 How do I report a bug? ▾
Select the "Bug Report" chip above and describe what happened, what you expected, and which browser/device you were on. Screenshots help a lot — paste an image URL if you have one. We fix confirmed bugs fast.
💡 Can I suggest a new feature? ▾
Absolutely — feature requests are our favourite messages. Pick "Feature Request" and describe the idea in as much detail as you like. Popular suggestions get prioritised in our roadmap.
🤝 Do you accept collaboration or sponsorship requests? ▾
Yes — we're open to content collaborations, affiliate partnerships, and brand sponsorships that are a genuine fit for anime fans. Select "Collaboration" and tell us about yourself and the opportunity. We review every request.
⏰ How long does it take to get a reply? ▾
We aim to reply within 24 hours on weekdays and within 48 hours on weekends. Bug reports and collaboration requests may take slightly longer as they need more review. You'll also receive an automatic confirmation email as soon as you submit.
🔒 Is my data safe when I submit this form? ▾
Yes. Your name, email, and message are only used to respond to your inquiry and to send the auto-reply confirmation. We don't sell or share your data. Full details in our Privacy Policy.
