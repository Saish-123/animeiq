Who We Are

Powering smarter anime discovery

AnimeIQ is a passion project turned platform, designed for the anime community. We pull real-time data from Jikan (MyAnimeList) to give you accurate scores, episode guides, filler maps, studio info, and more — all wrapped in a dark, fast, beautiful UI.

Whether you're a first-timer or a seasoned otaku who's watched 500 series, AnimeIQ meets you where you are. No ads nagging you. No subscriptions required. Just anime, instantly.

Our Journey

From idea to platform

The Frustration
Too many fillers, too little time

Like every anime fan, we got burned. Sitting through 60 episodes of Naruto filler that added nothing. We wanted a tool that said: "skip this — here's why." It didn't exist. So we built it.

The Build
AnimeIQ v1 goes live

Starting with a simple search + filler guide, we launched the first version. The response from the community was immediate and overwhelming — thousands of users in the first month.

The Growth
Watchlist, accounts & more

Firebase authentication, personal watchlists, favourites, and the Binge Calculator were added after user feedback. AnimeIQ became less a lookup tool and more a full companion app.

The Future
AnimeIQ Pro — coming soon

Ad-free experience, cross-device watchlist sync, community reviews, recommendation engine, and a native mobile app. The best is genuinely yet to come.
