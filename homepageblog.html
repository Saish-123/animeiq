What Are Filler Episodes?

Filler episodes are anime-only content not based on the original manga. AnimeIQ helps you identify them instantly so you never waste time on them again.

📺
What Is Filler?

Filler episodes are created by studios when the anime catches up to the manga. They're not canon and often add nothing to the main plot — but they waste your time.
